How multichannel event promotion works?

The whole multichannel event promotion is broken down in 6 steps, which are:

  1. you create an account, log in to your dashboard and create your event.
  2. Our team goes through your event, approve it for promotion and then send the details to all our channel partners.
  3. Once an event is live, the booking starts and people purchase the tickets.
  4. You check all the sales, RSVP, inquiries in Ercess Live dashboard and connects with your audience as and when necessary.
  5. Once the event is started, Ercess Live collects all the money from all the channel partners.
  6. Ercess Live transfers favored financial balance to the organizerâ€™s bank account within 5 business days after the event starts.
How will I know if my event is promoted?

When an event is approved by our team to promote, you receive an email alert. After this, you can log in to your account, go to Manage events > click on the event and you see a table which displays on which websites we are posting your event along with the current status. In order to cross verify, we provide you the public link of your event.

Why my payment is not being released?

There are two possibilities:

  1. You have not added your bank account details.
  2. You have not signed the agreement.
Can I get partial payment before event starts?

Yes, we can initiate the partial payment which is limited to 40% of all ticket sales.

Do you charge for free events?

No.
